# Sample Queries for RAG System Testing

## Clear, Well-Documented Questions (Should have high confidence):
1. How many vacation days do employees get after 3 years?
2. What is the company match percentage for 401k contributions?
3. What are the standard work hours at TechCorp?
4. How long is the parental leave for primary caregivers?
5. What is the minimum internet speed required for remote work?

## Edge Cases / Ambiguous Questions (May have lower confidence):
6. Can I work from home every day?
7. What happens if I'm late to work frequently?
8. How much can I spend on professional development?
9. Do I need approval to install software on my laptop?
10. What if I want to take more than 25 vacation days?

## Questions Not Covered in Documents (Should trigger hallucination detection):
11. What is our policy on cryptocurrency trading during work hours?
12. Can employees bring pets to the office?
13. What are the salary ranges for different positions?
14. Do we have a policy on artificial intelligence usage?
15. What is the process for reporting workplace harassment?

## Complex Questions Requiring Multiple Sources:
16. What benefits am I eligible for if I work remotely full-time?
17. If I have 5 years experience, how much vacation can I take and what professional development budget do I have?
18. What are all the requirements to become eligible for remote work?
19. What insurance options are available and when can I enroll?
20. What disciplinary actions might result from violating the remote work security policies?

## Intentionally Problematic Questions (Testing error handling):
21. [Empty question]
22. How much does the CEO make?
23. What's the best restaurant near the office?
24. Can you help me with my personal tax filing?
25. What's today's weather forecast?
