# Employee Handbook - TechCorp Inc.

## Company Overview
TechCorp Inc. is a leading software development company founded in 2018. We specialize in enterprise SaaS solutions and employ over 250 people globally.

## Work Hours and Attendance
- Standard work hours: 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM, Monday through Friday
- Flexible start times between 8:00 AM and 10:00 AM are permitted with manager approval
- All employees must work a minimum of 40 hours per week
- Lunch breaks are unpaid and must be at least 30 minutes
- Remote work is available 2 days per week after 90-day probationary period

## Code of Conduct
- Professional behavior is expected at all times
- Harassment, discrimination, or bullying will not be tolerated
- Confidential information must be protected at all times
- Personal use of company equipment should be minimal and appropriate
- Social media posts should not reflect negatively on the company

## Leave Policy  
- Vacation days: 15 days per year for employees with 0-2 years experience
- Vacation days: 20 days per year for employees with 3-5 years experience
- Vacation days: 25 days per year for employees with 6+ years experience
- Sick leave: 10 days per year (does not roll over)
- Parental leave: 12 weeks paid for primary caregiver, 4 weeks for secondary caregiver

## Disciplinary Actions
- Verbal warning for minor infractions
- Written warning for repeated or serious violations
- Final written warning before termination
- Immediate termination for gross misconduct including theft, violence, or breach of confidentiality

## Technology and Security
- All company devices must use approved antivirus software
- Passwords must be at least 12 characters with special characters
- Two-factor authentication is required for all business applications
- Personal software installations require IT department approval
- Data backup procedures must be followed weekly
