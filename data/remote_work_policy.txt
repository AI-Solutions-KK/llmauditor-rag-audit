# Remote Work Policy - TechCorp Inc.

## Eligibility Requirements
- Employees must complete 90-day probationary period before remote work eligibility
- Good performance standing required (last review rating of "Meets Expectations" or higher)
- Job role must be suitable for remote work (as determined by manager and HR)
- Reliable internet connection with minimum 25 Mbps upload/download speed required
- Dedicated workspace free from distractions

## Remote Work Schedule Options
- Hybrid schedule: 2-3 days remote per week (most common)
- Full remote: Available for exceptional circumstances with VP approval
- Temporary remote work: Available for medical reasons, weather, or family emergencies
- Core collaboration hours: Must be available 10:00 AM - 2:00 PM EST for meetings
- Time zone restrictions: Must work within 3 hours of EST

## Equipment and Technology
- Company laptop provided for all remote work
- Monitor, keyboard, mouse available upon request
- Internet stipend: $50/month for reliable broadband connection
- Home office setup allowance: $500 one-time payment for desk and chair
- VPN access mandatory for all company systems
- Company phone or phone stipend provided for customer-facing roles

## Communication Requirements
- Daily check-ins with immediate supervisor required
- Slack availability during core business hours mandatory
- Video calls preferred over audio-only for team meetings
- Response time expectation: 4 hours maximum during business hours
- Weekly one-on-one meetings with manager (can be virtual)

## Performance and Accountability
- Same performance standards apply for remote and in-office work
- Project deadlines and deliverables must be met consistently
- Regular productivity check-ins with supervisor
- Quarterly in-person meetings required for full remote employees
- Performance improvement plan may limit remote work privileges

## Security and Compliance
- All company data must remain secure and confidential
- Personal devices cannot be used for company work
- Screen locks must activate after 10 minutes of inactivity  
- Sensitive documents cannot be printed on personal printers
- Video call backgrounds must be professional or blurred
- Childcare arrangements required during work hours (children cannot be primary focus)

## Office Access and Collaboration
- Badge access maintained for all remote employees
- Desk hoteling system for hybrid workers
- Required office attendance: All-hands meetings, quarterly planning, team events
- Advance notice preferred for planned office visits
- Parking passes available for remote employees visiting office
