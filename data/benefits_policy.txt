# Benefits Policy - TechCorp Inc.

## Health Insurance
- Company covers 80% of employee premium costs
- Family coverage available with employee contributing 40% of additional premium
- Dental and vision coverage included in base health plan
- Health Savings Account (HSA) available with $500 annual company contribution
- Open enrollment period: November 1-30 each year
- New employees have 30 days from start date to enroll

## Retirement Benefits
- 401(k) plan available immediately upon hire
- Company match: 50% of first 6% contributed (3% maximum match)
- Vesting schedule: 20% per year, fully vested after 5 years
- Investment options include index funds, target-date funds, and ESG options
- Financial planning services provided by Fidelity at no cost
- Automatic enrollment at 3% contribution rate (can opt out)

## Professional Development
- $2,000 annual budget per employee for conferences, courses, and certifications
- Tuition reimbursement up to $5,000 per year for job-related degree programs
- Required pre-approval for all tuition reimbursement requests
- LinkedIn Learning subscription provided to all employees
- Internal mentorship program available
- Technical book allowance: $300 per year

## Additional Benefits
- Life insurance: 2x annual salary provided by company
- Disability insurance: Short-term (60% salary) and long-term (60% salary) coverage
- Employee assistance program for counseling and support services
- Commuter benefits: $150/month for public transportation or parking
- Gym membership reimbursement: Up to $50/month with receipts
- Free breakfast and lunch provided in office Monday-Thursday

## Stock Options
- Stock option grants for employees after 1 year of employment
- Grant amount based on performance review and role level
- 4-year vesting schedule with 1-year cliff
- Exercise price set at fair market value on grant date
- Post-termination exercise period: 90 days (3 months)

## Family Benefits
- Adoption assistance: Up to $5,000 reimbursement for adoption expenses
- Child care assistance: $500/month dependent care FSA contribution
- Elder care resources through employee assistance program
- Backup child care service: 20 days per year at subsidized rates
- Lactation support: Private nursing rooms and pumping equipment provided
